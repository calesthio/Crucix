## Goal
Turn source performance measurement into an operator workflow surface with attribution explanations, confidence caveats, and validation views tied to cluster quality and trust outcomes.

## Changes shipped
- Extended `lib/source-ops-runtime.mjs` so `sourceOps.performance` now exposes:
  - `version = "source-performance-workflow-v1"`
  - per-source `attributionExplanation` lists
  - per-source `confidenceCaveats`
  - `attributionCoverage` ratios for cluster attribution, signal coverage, and tipping-point coverage
  - workflow-level `confidenceCaveats`
  - workflow-level `validationViews` for cluster quality, review pressure, and trust outcomes

- Extended the operator settings/source console contract in `server.mjs` with `sourceConsole.performanceWorkflow`, including workflow summary counts, attribution headlines, caveats, validation views, and top-impact-source data.
- Updated `dashboard/public/source-ops.html` to surface:

  - validation views tied to cluster quality, review pressure, and trust outcomes
