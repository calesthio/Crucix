## Policy encoded by the contract

- Contract mode is file-first.
- Source-ops subagents are bounded by explicit role and task packets.
- Production mutations are disallowed from the source-ops contract.
- Auto-advance is allowed only through pre-production stages up to `shadow`.
- Human approval is still required before any source can be promoted to `active`.
