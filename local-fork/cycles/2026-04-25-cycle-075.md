## What changed

- Added a dedicated overlap rubric at `source-ops/overlap-rubric.json` with four weighted dimensions:
  - incremental corroboration value
  - duplication risk
  - coverage expansion
  - likely LLM utility
- Added a structured overlap assessment schema at `source-ops/schemas/overlap-assessment.schema.json`.
- Added an example overlap assessment at `source-ops/results/overlap/example-overlap-maritime-001.json` showing:
  - compared production sources
  - duplicate-signal risk
  - incremental coverage judgment
  - operator impact expectations
  - explainable weighted scoring
- Extended `source-ops/profile.json` with canonical paths for:

- Extended `lib/source-scorecard.mjs` with overlap-rubric and overlap-assessment summary helpers.
